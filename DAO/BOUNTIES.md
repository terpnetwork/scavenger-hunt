# TerpNET bounties

Submit a PR to this repository, adding your information to one of the bounties tables below. In the PR be sure to explain what you did 
and provide links to your documentation work.

TerpNET DAO members then will review your work and determine compensation, and if successful, merge your PR. Payout will happen in a 
timely manner after approval.

_No bounty rewards are certain until futher otherwise noted by Foundation of Terp Network._

## Documentation Bounties

_X Terp Tokens_ has been set aside to fund documentation efforts for [Terpnet documentation](https://docs.terp.network/).

See the [Terpnetwork/docs repository](https://github.com/terpnetwork/docs) and the open [Documentation 
v1.0](https://github.com/InterWasm/DAO/issues/35) issue for ideas on how and where to contribute.
